My Projects

My school assignment projects, and also my personal side projects.

AI generated art Classifier

A classifier model to detect and classify artwork produced by AI model. This is also part of my final year project for AI art detection learning. This model is trained by Anime arts from Pixiv including real human artworks and AI artworks. The classifier model is trained through Keras CNN method. A build in classifier web app is provided, try it out!

Wood Defect Detector

One of my group assignment of school projects for digital image processing course. It uses openCV for image processing and algorithms to detect the defects like holes and knots of wood in an image.
